Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I compress images for web use?

Compressing images reduces their file size, which speeds up your website. Faster websites provide a better user experience and rank higher on search engines.

Will compressing images reduce their quality?

Our tool uses advanced algorithms to minimize file size while maintaining the highest possible image quality. You can adjust the compression level to find the perfect balance.

What image formats can I compress?

Our tool supports popular formats such as JPEG, PNG, and GIF.

Is there a limit to the number of images I can compress?

You can compress as many images as you need. There are no restrictions on the number of images.

How long does the compression process take?

The compression process is quick, usually taking just a few seconds per image, depending on the file size and your internet connection.

Is my image data secure?

Absolutely. We prioritize your privacy and security. All images are processed securely and are not stored on our servers after compression.
